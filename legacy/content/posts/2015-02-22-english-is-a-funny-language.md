### IC &#8212; I See &#8212; Icy

  * IC : shorthand for &#8216;Intensive Care.&#8217;
  * I See : as in, &#8216;I see you.&#8217;
  * Icy : as in, &#8216;In Ohio, where I grew up, the winters were cold and icy.&#8217;
